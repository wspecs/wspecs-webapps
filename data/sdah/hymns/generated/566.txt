566 - Father, We Thank You


1
Father, We Thank You For The Light
That Shines All The Day;
For The Bright Sky You Have Given
Most Like Your Heaven;
Father, We Thank You.

2
Father, We Thank You For The Lamps
That Lighten The Way;
For Human Skill'S Exploration
Of Your Creation;
Father, We Thank You.

3
Father, We Thank You For The Friends
Who Brighten Our Play;
For Your Command To Call Others
Sisters And Brothers;
Father, We Thank You.

4
Father, We Thank You For Your Love
In Jesus Today,
Giving Us Hope For Tomorrow
Through Joy And Sorrow;
Father, We Thank You.
